Flipped

About the project

We came up with 'flipped teaching'- the idea to make students teach teachers. The huge leap in technology in recent years has made it that, for the first time in history, students can have more knowledge and skills than their teachers/lecturers. This is true especially in terms of technical skills. Our aim with this project is to leverage the skills of students to bring the skills of lecturers into the 21st century.

Weâ€™re working to implement it before post-COVID rebound since we can provide the new upskilling opportunity for teachers, but also help students to grow even despite the more difficult situation on the job market now.

While running our project from different universities (Lancaster, Bath & KCL) we already realized there is a need for a flexible approach as the universities' structure differs. We are open to collaborations with already existing institutions, as the main aim for us is to make use of our idea for the benefit of society.
